Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino dish featuring chicken braised in a flavorful blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper. It’s savory, tangy, and best served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken pieces and brown them on all sides.
  4. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Do not stir.
  5. Add bay leaves, black peppercorns, and sugar if using.
  6. Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  7. Uncover and let the sauce reduce to desired consistency.
  8. Garnish with green onions and serve with steamed rice.
