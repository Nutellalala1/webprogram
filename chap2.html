Stranger Things

Overview

Stranger Things is a science fiction-horror web television series created by the Duffer Brothers. The series takes place in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, in the 1980s and follows the story of a young boy who vanishes under mysterious circumstances.

Cast

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson

Characters

  • Mike Wheeler: The leader of the group of kids who become embroiled in the supernatural events.
  • Eleven: A young girl with psychokinetic abilities who escapes from a government laboratory.
  • Dustin Henderson: A curious and lovable member of the group of kids.

Reviews
